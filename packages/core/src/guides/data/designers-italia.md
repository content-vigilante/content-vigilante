# Designers Italia — Italian brand voice reference

## Source

[Designers Italia](https://designers.italia.it/) is the official design system of the Italian government, maintained by the Department for Digital Transformation (Presidenza del Consiglio dei Ministri) and AgID. The writing rules in `designers-italia.json` are extracted from the **Fondamenti** section:

- `/design-system/fondamenti/linguaggio/` — language and inclusive writing
- `/design-system/fondamenti/microtesti/` — microcopy (CTA, errors, confirmations, empty states)
- `/design-system/fondamenti/contenuti/` — content structure, headings, lists, links
- `/design-system/fondamenti/tono-di-voce/` — tone of voice and emotional modulation

## Why this source

It is the most-cited public Italian writing reference, has clear institutional ownership, and is published under a permissive license. Corporate Italian brand guides are either private or not published in a structured way; Designers Italia is the closest equivalent to Mailchimp's style guide for Italian.
